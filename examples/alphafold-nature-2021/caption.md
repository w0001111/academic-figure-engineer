# Caption

**Figure. Evidence-traceable reconstruction of the high-level AlphaFold architecture.** Sequence, multiple sequence alignment (MSA), and structural template information are transformed into learned MSA and pair representations. The Evoformer reasoning block exchanges information across these representations, after which recycling provides iterative refinement before the structure module predicts 3D coordinates and confidence estimates. This SVG is an original educational reconstruction based on the public method description of Jumper et al. (*Nature*, 2021) and does not reproduce any original paper figure.
