# Citation

This example is an original SVG reconstruction based on the publicly available method description of:

Jumper, J. et al. Highly accurate protein structure prediction with AlphaFold. *Nature* 596, 583–589 (2021). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-021-03819-2

The figure in this example is **not** a copy, tracing, or reproduction of any original figure from the paper. It is an educational case study created to demonstrate evidence-traceable academic figure generation.

Access and impact information observed from the Nature article page when this example was prepared: the article was open access and listed more than three million accesses, over forty-five thousand citations, and an Altmetric score above four thousand.
